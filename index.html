About Me

I am passionate about helping small businesses build their presence on the web.

I started taking Skillcrush classes, learning to design and code beautiful websites, in order to help small business owner's design a space that's all their own. Having a web presence is a 'must' these days and those drag-and-drop editors aren't always easy (and how often can you really make it look the way you want?). So, I decided to learn how to code and help other entrepreneurs like myself build spaces they're proud of that showcase the best of their brand.

Can't wait to work with you!
